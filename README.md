📊 HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard
📌 Project Overview

This project focuses on analyzing employee attrition data to identify key factors influencing employee turnover. Using interactive visualizations in Power BI, the dashboard helps HR teams make data-driven decisions to improve employee retention.

🎯 Objectives
Analyze employee attrition trends
Identify key factors contributing to employee turnover
Provide actionable insights for HR decision-making
Build an interactive and user-friendly dashboard
🛠️ Tools & Technologies
Power BI – Data visualization and dashboard creation
Excel / CSV – Data source
DAX – Calculations and measures
Power Query – Data cleaning and transformation
📂 Dataset Description

The dataset includes employee-related information such as:

Age
Gender
Department
Job Role
Salary / Income
Years at Company
Job Satisfaction
Attrition (Yes/No)
📊 Key Dashboard Features
📈 Attrition Overview (Total Employees, Attrition Rate)
👥 Attrition by Department & Job Role
💰 Attrition by Salary Range
📅 Attrition by Years at Company
😊 Job Satisfaction Analysis
⚖️ Gender-based Attrition Insights
🔍 Key Insights
Higher attrition observed among employees with lower salaries
Employees with fewer years at the company are more likely to leave
Certain job roles show significantly higher turnover
Job satisfaction plays a critical role in employee retention
💡 Business Recommendations
Improve compensation for low-income employees
Focus on employee engagement in early years
Provide career growth opportunities
Enhance workplace satisfaction and culture
